A story of Drama and Deceit

There was once a penguine. He was waddle through Antarctica looking for his next meal. It had been 2 days since his last fish and he had lost his way.

He had wandered far and wide, yet found himself wihout a fish. He thought Maybe the polar bears ate all the fish. He looked out into the distance and saw what looked like a wolf. Oh shoot

The penguine decided to hide in a hole nearby

The dark figure approached the area that the penguin was hiding. He peeked over the top of the hole and saw what he thought was a wolf

The penguin realized it was only a dog and hopefully would not eat him, so he stepped out of his hole and said, "What's up?"

"Nothing. I'm out here exploring."

"Do you have any food kind sir?"

"I am a woman."

"I'll trade you a fish for an egg."

"I'm a man. I don't have eggs."

"I know all about penguin culture. The male carries the egg."

The penguin pulled his egg out from under his belly.

The penguin "accidently" dropped his egg. It fell towards some rocks nearby and he picked it back up. He handed the dog the egg. The dog said, "Thanks, here's a fish."


The penguin quickly ate the fish and started on his journey again
I hope he doesn't notice
